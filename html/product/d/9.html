Purpose 

This kit allows for the determination of ADP concentrations in Human serum,    blood plasma, and other biological fluids  . 

Principle of the assay 

T  he kit assay Human ADP level in the sample  ，  use Purified Human ADP antibody    to coat microtiter plate wells, make solid-phase antibody, then add ADP to wells,  Combined ADP antibody which With HRP labeled  ，  become antibody-antigen- enzyme  antibody complex, after washing Completely, Add TMB substrate solution, TMB    substrate becomes blue color At HRP enzyme-catalyzed, reaction is terminated by the    addition of a sulphuric acid solution and the color change is measured    spectrophotometrically at a wavelength of 450 nm. The concentration of ADP in the    samples is then determined by comparing the O.D. of the samples to the standard curve.
